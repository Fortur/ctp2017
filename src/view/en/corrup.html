Subsection Disclosure of Anti-Corruption Activities

All companies which belong to Premium II level of transparency, following the international standards of the disclosure of non-financial information, must have a public position in the field of combating corruption and publish such information in their report. 45 companies have regulations, policies and procedures for preventing corruption. 43 companies have special channels through which anyone can pass the information about corruption, but the persons responsible for this have been appointed only in 36 companies. Only 36 largest Russian companies reported on the implementation of anti-corruption programs in the reporting period. This fact shows that the rest of the companies failed either to implement anti-corruption programs or to disclose the information about their implementation.
