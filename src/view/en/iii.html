The companies belonging to the third level of transparency do not use international guidelines and international standards while preparing reports. As a rule, the system of public reporting of such companies is poorly developed. The most common element is the regulatory framework for the public reporting and disclosure. Only 6 out of 147 such companies used the method of cooperation with stakeholders in the course of the preparation of reports: IDGC of Urals, PJSC TMK, PJSC Enel Russia, JSC ZiO, JSC Fazer. Only 5 companies out of 147 conducted an internal audit of non-financial information: PJSC "DEK", PJSC "Mordovia Energy Retail Company", JSC "Rusagro Group", JSC "Ilim Group", PJSC "Tattelecom".

Nevertheless, even the third level companies are concerned about the ease of use of reporting. The release of electronic (interactive) reports by 16 companies proves this fact. One third of the companies (46 out of 147 companies, 31%) maintain minimal channels of swift communication with stakeholders, providing the contacts of the persons responsible for the communication concerning the disclosure the data in the reports.
