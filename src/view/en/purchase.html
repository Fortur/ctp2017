Disclosure of Procurement Activities

Disclosure of information on procurement activities

The most of the companies disclosed some data about purchasing activities (at least some brief information about one of the subjects of this subsection). This is true for all 48 companies of the leading group (Premium, I and II levels of transparency) which published the data on their official websites. 30 companies are state-owned, and therefore their procurement activities are regulated by Federal Law No. 233-FZ of July 18, 2011 (as amended on June 29, 2015).

The number of the leading group companies, which disclosed the information about their procurement(2016-2017)

All 48 leading group companies published the information on procurement activities on the official website, but only 43 out of 48 companies provided their documents regulating procurement activities (regulations / policies / procedures), 38 - confirmed the availability of the internal control over purchases. An even a smaller number of the companies (35 companies out of 48) disclosed the information on the procurement plans for the next reporting period.

It should be noted that 48 out of 52 (92%) leading group companies published the information on the procurement activities in 2016, while this year 100% of the leading group companies have disclosed this information. There have been positive changes in some other fields related to the transparency of procurement.
