Usability of Reporting and Efficient Communication Channels with Stakeholders

Reporting usability

28 out of 48 leading companies (Premium, I and II transparency levels) submitted electronic (online) annual reports, available on the Internet. 19 of them adopted this format to various mobile devices - interactive reports of companies are adapted to small screens of tablets and smartphones.

The number of the companies providing convenient reporting (2016-2017)

Mobile applications is not a common practice (6 companies). They are usually available on Google Play and App Store.

A great number of Russian companies (47 of 48) provided references to other information sources, thereby reducing the volume of the report and increasing the usability of the information.

36 out of 48 companies care about the convenience of studying reports and add hyperlinks to other sections of the report in pdf-versions of their reporting documents.

Though almost all of the companies included the sections dedicated to abbreviations and terms in their reports, some companies still ignore such appliances and show their careless attitude to report users. However, there is a positive trend compared to the last year to include a glossary and a list of abbreviations in the reports.

Efficient communication channels with stakeholders

The leading companies (Premium, I and II transparency levels) are developing efficient communication channels with their stakeholders: 46 out of 48 companies have official accounts in at least one of the social networks (Facebook, Twitter, Vkontakte etc.). Channels for operational communication with stakeholders (2016-2017)

Almost all the companies (46 out of 48 companies) provided the contacts with the service responsible for the information disclosure.

Companies gradually develop all possible channels of efficient communication with stakeholders (a greater expansion of these channels and facilities as compared to the previous reporting period). This allows to enhance the requesting process on the part of the stakeholders as well as to select the important data (significant subjects) to be disclosed in the reports.
