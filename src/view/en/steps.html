Study stages:

  1. Survey of the reporting and official websites of companies:
    • the first stage – 956 companies were surveyed. The companies which do not publish reports, or publish reports only in a foreign language, or publish only the traditional annual reports (under the requirements of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation) were not included in the second stage;
    • the second stage – 182 companies were surveyed based on the criteria. The basic survey procedure – scanning of reports and corporate websites through the "information grid": criteria / parameters / indicators (10 criteria, 95 parameters, 141 indicators for the companies which used international reporting standards and 10 criteria, 23 parameters, 30 indicators – for the companies which did not use international reporting standards, but disclosed information at the level exceeding the requirements of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation).
  2. Spot-check the results of companies' survey by the Expert group (15% of companies were checked – 27 companies).
  3. Verification (approval) of received data with the companies (48 companies took part in the verification of preliminary results).
  4. Audit for compliance of the conducted study with the methodology.
  5. Conclusions.
